About the Owner

I am proud to be the owner and a hairstylist at Infinity Locks. I have been in the industry for 5 years now. I love my job because I get to make every person that sits in my chair feel great about themselves. I get to meet so many new people and everyone has a different story, wants a different look and I get to help them accomplish it. I LOVE to go to classes and learn the latest trends and styles and bring them back to my clients.Doing sisterlocks to me is one of the most rewarding services that I have done. It can change a person's life.
