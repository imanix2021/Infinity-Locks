WELCOME

Sisterlocks, founded by Dr. Joanne Cornwell, is a hair care system that allows you to wear your natural curly hair without altering the natural texure of your hair without chemicals.

Considering Sisterlocks? Look no futher, certified Sisterlocks consultant Valerie "Val" Curtis cares about the integrity of your CROWN. Val received her Sisterlocks certification February of 2016 in Augusta, Georgia. Sisterlocks start by utilizing a precision grid pattern, and the use of a specialized tool to install the hair into its locking formation. Passionate and understanding of each client's hair journy, Val provides a solace atmosphere with one on one consultations where you will become empowered and confident with the knowlegde of your new establishment or providing maintanence for your current. Sisterlocks are a lifetime change and a true commitment. Let Val help you embrace this unique hair lifestyle with confidence, poise, and patience that only a certified Sisterlock consultant can give. On behalf of the Sisterlock community we welcome you and celebrate you on your new journey!
